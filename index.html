HOW TO PLAY: Simply drag and drop the pieces onto the grid to make vertical and horizontal lines.

When you make a line it disappears! The more lines you clear at once, the more points you get!

You can place Flashing Pieces onto existing pieces to make them disappear!

Just make sure you have enough room to place new pieces, or you lose!

Created by Daniel Prilik. Conceptually similar to 1010! by Gram Games. Aesthetically inspired by 2048 by Gabriele Cirulli.

You can check out the source code on my GitHub!
